Kickball and Soccer Late Registration
Did you miss 3v3 soccer or kickball registration or forget to take your captains exam before the deadline? Kickball and 3v3 soccer late registration is now open. A trip to the office is required and there you will fill out the late registration form and receive the password for the exam.
How to Find Your Schedule
TeamSideline has several ways for you to find your schedule.  Click on the "+" next to your sport in the lefthand sidebar to find it by day, time, or division.  Or, simply click on "Find Team Schedule" in the upper left and search by your team name.  When you visit our mobile site from your phone, you can do a quick schedule search too!
